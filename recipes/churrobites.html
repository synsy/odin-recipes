Churro Bites

These are very close to the regular deep fried churros. Serve with hot chocolate, or dipped in a chocolate sauce.

Ingredients

  • Water
  • Butter
  • Brown Sugar
  • Salt
  • Flour
  • Vanilla Extract
  • Eggs

Steps

  1. Combine water, butter, brown sugar, and salt.
  2. Add vanilla extract and eggs.
  3. Preheat oven to 425 degrees F.
  4. Transfer dough to pastry bag.
  5. Pipe pastries onto a cookie sheet.
  6. Bake for 10 minutes.
