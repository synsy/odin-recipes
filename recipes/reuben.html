Reuben Sandwich

A Reuben is a grilled sandwich featuring corned beef on rye bread. The Reuben is a menu staple in Jewish-style delicatessens, but it's not technically kosher because it combines meat and cheese.

Ingredients

  • Rye Bread
  • Thousand Island Dressing
  • Swiss Cheese
  • Corned Beef
  • Sauerkraut
  • Butter

Steps

  1. Preheat a griddle or skillet.
  2. Spread one side of bread slices evenly with thousand island dressing.
  3. Add swiss cheese.
  4. Add corned beef.
  5. Add sauerkraut.
  6. Grill the sandwich.
